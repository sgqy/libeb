Options for Information

When you invoke ebzipinfo with the --subbook and/or --skip-content options, ebzipinfo outputs information about files matched to the given condition only. See Partial Compression, for details about these options.

ebzipinfo ignores the following options; --force-overwrite, --keep, --level, --no-overwrite, --output-directory, --quiet, --silence and --test.
